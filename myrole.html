Detailed analysis of the principles that guide my academic and professional conduct.

I view responsibility as the foundation of any project. In my IGCSE Physics and Chemistry collaborations, I take full ownership of the project lifecycle, proactively identifying risks and ensuring every team member has the resources needed to succeed under pressure.

Leadership requires understanding the unique pressures faced by peers. During high-stress periods, such as an 8-exam week, I prioritize maintaining team morale and offering technical support to ensure no one falls behind the curriculum pace.
