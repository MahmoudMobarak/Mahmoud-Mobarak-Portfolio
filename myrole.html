Taking ownership of tasks and ensuring they are completed to a high standard has always been a priority, whether in academic work or team settings.

Empathy

Supporting others, including everyone, and understanding different perspectives allows me to build strong and respectful relationships.

Service

Contributing to the school community by helping peers and stepping forward when needed reflects my commitment to service.

Integrity
